About Dumki Upazila Students Association (DUSA)

Established on January 19, 2001, DUSA is an apolitical student organization uniting students from Dumki Upazila studying in Dhaka and beyond. We focus on networking, skill-building, academic support, and community service.

Our initiatives include talent hunts, admission workshops, meritorious receptions, magazines, iftar programs, tree plantations, picnics, and seminars to foster growth and unity.
